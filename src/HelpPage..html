Need Help with GoRide?

We've got answers to your frequently asked questions.

How do I book a bus ticket?

Simply search for your destination, select your preferred bus, choose your seat, and proceed to payment.

Can I cancel or reschedule a ticket?

Yes, go to 'My Bookings' and select the ticket you want to cancel or reschedule. Make sure to check our cancellation policy.

Is there a customer support number?

Yes, you can contact our 24/7 support team via the 'Contact Us' section in the app.
